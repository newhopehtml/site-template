Life Groups

New Hope life groups are small gatherings of people meeting weekly at homes and other places to be intentional about growing in their relationship with Jesus. We believe that growth happens in the context of relationships. We have a variety of groups meeting every day of the week and we would love to have you join a group! Simply choose a day that works best with your schedule, click on a group and sign up!

Men’s

The Men’s Ministry is a pillar of New Hope Chapel. Our mission is to encourage and equip men to know the Bible, teach it, and faithfully follow Christ as husbands, fathers, and friends. Our core program, Men’s Fraternity, gives us a definition of manhood from which to work, and a road on which to walk. In addition to the Men’s Fraternity and many weekly life groups, we are pleased to host the annual Teleios Men’s Summit. This large conference helps men to be conformed to the image of God in all areas of their lives by connecting with God and with other men.
